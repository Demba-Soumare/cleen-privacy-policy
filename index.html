Introduction

Cleen ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Cleen Cart browser extension.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • Product Information: Titles, prices, images, URLs, and descriptions from websites you choose to save
  • Website URLs: URLs of product pages you add to your cart
  • Price History: Historical price data for monitored products
  • Timestamps: Dates and times when products are added or prices are checked

User-Provided Information

  • Manual product entries and notes
  • Settings and preferences (language, theme, notifications)

How We Use Your Information

  • Display saved products in your centralized cart
  • Monitor and notify price changes
  • Provide analytics and price history
  • Sync across devices (if Firebase sync is enabled)

Data Storage

Local Storage

By default, all your data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's Storage API. It never leaves your device unless you enable cloud sync.

Cloud Storage (Optional)

If you enable Firebase cloud sync:

  • Your data is stored in Google Firebase / Firestore
  • Data is anonymous and encrypted via HTTPS/TLS
  • No personal information is required

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT sell, share, or use your data for advertising or marketing.

Third-Party Services:

  • Firebase / Firestore (Google): Used only for optional sync, subject to Google‚Äôs Privacy Policy.
  • E-commerce websites: Accessed only to extract product data. Your cart is not shared with them.

Permissions Explained

  • activeTab: Extract product data
  • storage: Save products locally
  • tabs: Open and check product pages
  • scripting: Inject scripts to read product data
  • notifications: Notify price changes
  • alarms: Schedule price checks
  • host_permissions: Support all e-commerce websites

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and view your data directly in the extension
  • Export your data (JSON format)
  • Delete products or clear all data
  • Disable features or uninstall to delete data

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Retained until manually deleted or uninstalled
  • Cloud Data: Retained until deleted through the extension or on request

Security

  • Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Anonymous authentication
  • Strict Firebase security rules
  • No server-side credentials

GDPR Compliance (European Users)

As a European developer based in France, Agence224 ensures compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent anytime by disabling sync or uninstalling

Contact us at agence224.web@gmail.com to exercise your rights.

Children's Privacy

Cleen Cart is not directed to children under 13 and does not collect data from them.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically and will notify users through the extension.

International Data Transfers

If you enable cloud sync, your data may be stored on Firebase servers worldwide. Firebase complies with GDPR and the EU-U.S. Data Privacy Framework.

Technical Details (Transparency)

We Access: Product metadata (title, price, image, URL) and current prices during checks.

We Do NOT Access: Browsing history, payment info, personal accounts, or unrelated websites.

Data Format: Structured JSON, exportable anytime.
